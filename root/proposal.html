Portfolio Website Proposal

Purpose of my Website

The purpose of my website will be to showcase some of the artwork that I have made over the years.

Target Audience

The target audience for my website is clients looking for artwork to be created for personal use in the form of commissions. My main target audience will be independent individuals looking for art rather than any company, since I do not believe that I am at appropriate skill level to make artworks outside of a niche audience.

Required Portfolio Components

  • Artwork Preview
  • Pricing
  • Policy on Refunds
  • Contact Information
  • Avaliability
